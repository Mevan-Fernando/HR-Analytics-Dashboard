# HR Analytics Dashboard — Power BI

## Overview
An interactive 3-page Power BI dashboard analyzing employee attrition 
patterns across 1,470 employees using the IBM HR Analytics dataset.

**Main question answered:** Why are employees leaving — and what should 
the company do about it?

---

## Key Findings
- 16.1% overall attrition rate — costing an estimated **$1.70M/month**
- **Sales Representatives** show the highest role attrition at **40%**
- Overtime employees leave at **31%** vs 10% for non-overtime staff
- Employees earning under $4K/month show **25% attrition**
- 30% of employees leave within their **first 2 years**
- A 30% attrition reduction could save approximately **$692K annually**
